## What is KORDEAL?

KORDEAL is a B2B wholesale sourcing platform that bridges **Korean suppliers** and **global buyers** — think of it as the Korean equivalent of 1688.com, built for the world.

> *"Source from Korea, the way it should be."*

---

## Why KORDEAL?

- 🌍 **Global-first** — English & multilingual support, global payments
- 🔒 **Escrow protection** — Buyer & seller both protected on every transaction
- 📋 **Trade compliance guide** — Country × category regulation matrix built in
- 🏺 **Jangin Market** — Dedicated category for rare, handcrafted Korean artisan goods
- ⚡ **AI-powered onboarding** — Korean suppliers register in Korean, auto-translated to English

## Jangin Market (장인마켓)

A dedicated channel for **rare, handmade Korean artisan products** — pottery, metalcraft, silver jewelry, traditional lacquerware, and more.

Unlike mass-produced goods, every Jangin Market item carries a story and cannot be replicated.

---

## Escrow Payment Flow

```
Buyer pays → Escrow holds funds → Supplier ships
→ Buyer confirms receipt → Funds released to supplier
```

Dispute? Our mediation team resolves within 72 hours.
